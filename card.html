Emperor Penguin

The emperor is largest of all penguins.

  • Scientific Name: Aptenodytes forsteri
  • Average Length: 45 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 15-20 years
  • Habitat: Polar - Antarctic ice

Living on the Antarctic ice and in the frigid surrounding waters, emperor penguins have adapted to some of the harshest environments on earth. After the females lay a single egg, they take a two month hunting trip, leaving the males to huddle together to conserve warmth while they protect the eggs. When the females return with full bellies, they regurgitate the food to feed their young.
